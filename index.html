About Me

Hi! My name is Adam Gallina, and I enjoy building simple game proof-of-concepts. I've completed a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science at Oregon State University, with a focus in Simulation/Game Programming. I like to make games that don’t take themselves too seriously, but still provide a fun experience to the player.

I first began learning to make games in 2016 (2014 if you count Scratch…) when I joined my high school’s Game Dev team to compete in the Oregon Game Programming Challenge, and have continued learning since, making personal projects as well as submissions for various game jams.
